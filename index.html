Osama bin Laden

Introduction

Osama bin Laden was a Saudi Arabian-born extremist and the founder of the militant Islamist organization al-Qaeda. He was born on March 10, 1957, in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, into a wealthy and prominent family with strong connections to the Saudi royal family. Bin Laden gained international notoriety for his role in orchestrating the September 11, 2001, terrorist attacks on the United States.
